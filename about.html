Since 1993

32 Years Building Combat-Ready Forces

Zen Technologies Limited is headquartered in Hyderabad, India, with offices in the UAE and USA — engineering combat training and counter-drone systems for armed, paramilitary and police forces worldwide.

Vision

To be a leading player globally in providing Combat Training and Counter Drone solutions to Armed, Paramilitary, and Police Forces.

Mission

Delivering Combat Training and Anti-Drone solutions through innovative and cost-effective solutions.

Milestones

32 Years In Motion

Tap any milestone for more detail.

1993

Zen Technologies incorporated, beginning with simulation-based training systems for the Indian armed forces.

2000s

Growth into driving simulators, tank gunnery trainers and live-range instrumentation — the foundation of today's 100+ product portfolio.

2010s

Offices established in the UAE and USA; over 1,000 simulators shipped worldwide, laying the groundwork for exports to 60+ countries today.

2020s

Launch of the multi-layer Anti Drone System and the RCWS family — Parashu, Fanish, Sharur, Hawkeye and Prahasta.

2025–2026

CII Best Patent Portfolio Award, 180+ trademarks and 57+ design registrations on record, and continued build-out of the Combat Training Centre's Readiness Points scoring system.

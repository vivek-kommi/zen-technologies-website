Combat Training Centre

Zen's flagship system-of-systems. It integrates Live Ranges, Live Simulation, Virtual Simulation and constructive after-action tooling into one scalable environment — and outputs a single, comparable Combat Readiness Score per unit.

Flagship System Category: Plan → Train Platoon to Brigade scale

Why It Exists

One Score. Every Layer.

Units historically train across disconnected systems — a live range here, a simulator there — with no single measure of readiness. CTC unifies them into one environment and one number commanders can actually compare across units.

  • 01 Unifies Live Ranges, Live Simulation and Virtual Simulation under one environment
  • 02 Produces a single, comparable Combat Readiness Score per unit
  • 03 Scales from platoon-level exercises to full brigade-level manoeuvres
  • 04 Built-in after-action review and constructive simulation layer

Gamified By Design

Every Drill Earns Readiness Points

The Combat Readiness Score isn't one opaque number — it's built from Readiness Points earned in five categories, on every system, every session. Select a tier below to see what it takes to reach it.

Accuracy

Shot grouping and hit-detection data from LOMAH, MFTS and every simulator's scoring engine.

Speed

Time-to-engage and reaction benchmarks captured automatically, no manual stopwatch required.

Decision-Making

Scenario branching in force-on-force and virtual exercises scores judgement, not just marksmanship.

Teamwork

Squad and crew coordination across TacSim and ZEN ACTS force-on-force exercises.

Safety Compliance

Range and drill safety protocol adherence, tracked automatically on every live exercise.

Recruit — Baseline competency across all five point categories. Cleared for supervised live-range and simulator training.

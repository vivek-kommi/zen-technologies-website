Our Doctrine

Plan. Train. Execute. Repeat.

Every Zen system exists inside one continuous readiness loop — from mission planning, through instrumented training, to fielded hardware, back to the data that plans the next cycle.

01 / Plan

Plan

Design the exercise, set the standard, score what "ready" means before a single trainee steps on the range.

  • Combat Readiness Score benchmarking
  • Scenario & curriculum design
  • Unit-to-brigade exercise orchestration
02 / Train

Train

Build muscle memory across every domain — indoors, on the range, and in full force-on-force exercises.

  • Live Ranges & Live Simulation
  • Virtual Simulation, 40+ platforms
  • Combat Training Centre integration
03 / Execute

Execute

Field the same engineering discipline in real hardware — weapon stations and counter-drone systems on active duty.

  • RCWS across land, armour & naval
  • Multi-layer Anti Drone System
  • Autonomous & optronic platforms
04 / Repeat

Repeat

Every engagement earns Readiness Points. After-action data feeds straight back into the next planning cycle.

  • Automated after-action review
  • Points-based performance scoring, Recruit to Elite
  • Continuous readiness improvement
